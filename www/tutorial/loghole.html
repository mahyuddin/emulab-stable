Loghole - Emulab Log Management Utility

The loghole utility downloads log files from certain directories on the experimental nodes (e.g. "/local/logs") to the Emulab users machine. After downloading, it can also be used to produce and manage archives of the log files. Using this utility to manage an experiment's log files is encouraged because it will transfer the logs in a network-friendly manner and is already integrated with the rest of Emulab. For example, any programs executed using the Emulab event-system will have their standard output/error automatically placed in the "/local/logs" directory. The tool can also be used to preserve multiple trials of an experiment by producing and managing zip archives of the logs. You can learn more about the loghole utility by reading its man page on users.emulab.net.
